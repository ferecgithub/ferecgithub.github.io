ABOUT ME


Hi there! I'm Ferec Hamitbeyli from Baku, Azerbaijan. I'm currently doing master’s degree in information technologies in Ege University, İzmir. I'm a self-taught software developer who has chemistry background. I've always loved to create something virtual to have an impact on real lives. And then, I found my true calling in software development. I find it very important to grasp a topic from the group up, so I decided to study IT while also learning from different online sources. We live in such a wonderful era that everyone with enough motivation and who can understand English, can learn and accomplish whatever he or she desires.

I'm into mobile technologies and web technologies. Currently developing android applications with Kotlin and improving my knowledge modern android components (such as MVVM architecture, Android Jetpack etc.) and getting to know some Kotlin frameworks such as KTOR and TornadoFX. Beside programming, I also like to design my assets, character animations and material design.
